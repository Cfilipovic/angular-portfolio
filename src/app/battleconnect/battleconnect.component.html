About Battle Connect

BattleConnect is a mobile Android app for managing and visualizing IOBT (interet of battlefield things) data.

Submit reports from the battlefield. Receive important alerts from authorities and explore IOBT sensor data on the map around your current location. IOBT refers to battlefied devices that are connected in a similar manner as traditional IOT devices.
